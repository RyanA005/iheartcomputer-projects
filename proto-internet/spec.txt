IHCTP (IHeartComputer Transport Protocol)
v0.1

Runs over TCP on port 6700.
Text protocol (UTF-8).

--------------------------
REQUEST
--------------------------
GIVE <filename>\n

--------------------------
RESPONSE
--------------------------
FINE <data>\n
ERRO <msg>\n
